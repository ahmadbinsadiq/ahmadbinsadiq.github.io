I’m currently leading and developing several high-impact projects that integrate AI, data analytics, and human-centric transformation to shape the next generation of intelligent, adaptive, and Industry 5.0-driven systems.

1. Cost Analysis Automation (Hourly Cost Model)

Objective: Build an intelligent system to calculate real-time hourly production and operational cost per machine and per line.

  • Integrating data from production logs, energy meters, and manpower data.
  • Using Python and Power BI to automate cost visibility across departments.
  • Empowering leadership to make cost-based scheduling and pricing decisions.
Impact Goal: Improve cost transparency and profitability analysis by 30–40%.

2. AI–Human Collaboration Model for Changeover Optimization

Objective: Develop an AI-driven decision system that enhances human–machine collaboration to minimize changeover time and downtime losses by up to 40%.

  • Integrating machine learning with operator expertise to identify optimal deckle size, paper change sequence, and height adjustments in real time.
  • Leveraging AI-assisted scheduling algorithms to dynamically balance machine utilization and human workload.
  • Implementing an adaptive learning loop — continuously improving efficiency across production cycles.
  • Providing real-time insights for supervisors to make faster, data-driven decisions.
Impact Goal: Reduce changeover and downtime by up to 40% through seamless AI–Human collaboration.

3. Organizational Development & Coaching System

Objective: Create a leadership and development framework to strengthen collaboration and learning.

  • Coaching managers on Lean leadership and data-driven decision-making.
  • Building mentorship frameworks and visibility dashboards.
  • Linking people development metrics with operational KPIs.
Impact Goal: Build a self-learning organization that continuously adapts and evolves.

4. Daily Management System (DMS) for Industry 5.0

Objective: Develop a digital DMS combining shop-floor data, maintenance, and performance insights.

  • Designing daily KPI dashboards for OEE, downtime, and shift performance.
  • Embedding automated alerts, action logs, and escalation protocols.
  • Aligning DMS with Lean and AI-based continuous improvement principles.
Impact Goal: Create a transparent, adaptive, and human-centered management system aligned with Industry 5.0.

5. AI-Driven Decision Support Engine

Objective: Build an AI-powered decision assistant that recommends optimal daily actions for operational excellence.

  • Analyzes production, cost, and manpower data to generate insights.
  • Uses reinforcement learning to improve recommendations over time.
  • Integrates with Power BI and alert systems for proactive management.
Impact Goal: Reduce decision-making time by 50% and improve production alignment.

6. Intelligent Skills Matrix & Workforce Planning

Objective: Create a dynamic skills management platform merging HR analytics with operational data.

  • Tracks real-time skill progression and competency mapping.
  • Automatically recommends training, cross-skilling, and role assignments.
  • Provides predictive insights into workforce capacity and future needs.
Impact Goal: Enhance workforce capability by 25% through smarter training and utilization mapping.
